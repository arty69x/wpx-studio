# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We commit to a professional, respectful, inclusive, and productive environment for everyone contributing to this repository. Engineering quality depends on psychological safety, clear communication, and constructive disagreement.

## Expected Behavior

- Communicate with respect and assume good intent.
- Critique ideas, designs, code, and documents without attacking people.
- Welcome questions and explain decisions clearly.
- Share context that helps others contribute effectively.
- Accept responsibility for mistakes and correct them promptly.
- Respect different backgrounds, skill levels, time zones, and communication styles.

## Unacceptable Behavior

Harassment, discrimination, insults, threats, sexualized language, doxxing, sustained disruption, bad-faith reviews, and retaliation are not acceptable.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may clarify expectations, request edits, close discussions, reject contributions, restrict access, or escalate issues according to the severity and persistence of the behavior.

## Reporting

Report conduct concerns to repository maintainers with relevant links, timestamps, screenshots, and a concise description of the concern. Reports should be handled discreetly and fairly.
